About Testimony Sacco

A community built on trust, transparency and shared prosperity since 2019.

Our Story

Born from Community, Built for Community

Testimony Staff Savings and Credit Co-operative Society Limited was founded in 2019 by a group of dedicated staff members in Eldoret who believed in the power of collective savings and mutual support.

What started as a small savings group has grown into a registered co-operative serving hundreds of members, providing affordable credit and fostering financial discipline among its membership.

Registered under the Co-operative Societies Act of Kenya, Testimony Sacco operates with full transparency, democratic governance and unwavering commitment to its members' financial wellbeing.

Our Financial Services

Comprehensive financial solutions designed for every stage of your journey.

💰

Savings & Shares

Build your financial foundation with disciplined monthly savings. Every shilling saved is a shilling working for your future — earning dividends and growing your share capital year after year.

  • Minimum KES 1,000 per month
  • Annual dividend payments based on savings proportion
  • Shares accumulate at KES 1,000 per share
  • Track your balance anytime via the member portal
  • Use savings to offset loans when needed
Check My Balance →
🏦

Normal Loans

Access funds for personal development, business growth, home improvement or any purpose — at interest rates far below commercial banks. Backed by your savings and guaranteed by fellow members.

  • Borrow up to 3× your savings balance
  • Interest rate: 4.2% on reducing balance
  • Flexible repayment periods
  • Two guarantors required from active members
  • Processing within 5 working days
Apply Now →
⚡

Salary Advance

When your salary is days away but an urgent need cannot wait — the salary advance bridges the gap quickly, with minimal paperwork and fast approval.

  • Processed within 24 hours
  • Based on net salary — no guarantors needed
  • Repaid in full on next salary date
  • Available to all active members in good standing
Apply Now →

Emergency Fund

Life is unpredictable. Medical emergencies, accidents and unforeseen crises happen. The Emergency Fund ensures you are never left to face these moments alone or without resources.

  • Available for genuine emergencies only
  • Faster processing than normal loans
  • Medical, accident, disaster situations covered
  • Compassionate review process
Learn More →
🎓

School Fees Loan

Education is the best investment. Whether it is primary, secondary or university fees — the School Fees Loan ensures education is never interrupted by financial constraints.

  • Repayment aligned to school terms
  • Available for member's children or self-education
  • Student name and institution required
  • Competitive interest rate
Apply Now →
🤝

Benevolent Fund

In times of bereavement and extreme hardship, the Testimony Sacco Benevolent Fund provides solidarity support to members and their immediate families. Because no member should grieve alone.

  • Monthly contribution: KES 200 per member
  • Supports members during bereavement
  • Covers immediate family members
  • Claim processed within 48 hours of notification
